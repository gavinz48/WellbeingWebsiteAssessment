About Us

Onslow College's wellbeing programme is committed to providing a safe environment for all students. We wish for students to feel positive during their time at Onslow College. Please feel free to contact any of counsellors or staff for further help and information about us.

Here at Onslow College, we have a variety of programmes and forms of help for our students to suit their needs. We have in-school programmes including Ako Time, Manaakitanga@Onslow, and Peer Mediation. Online forms of help include emailing our staff and talking to our counsellors.
